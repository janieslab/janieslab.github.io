Datasets, Tools, and Dashboards

EchinoDB

Echinoderm Genomics

EchinoDB is a database consisting of amino acid sequence othoclusters from echinoderm transcriptomes. Taxa are sampled to span the deepest divergences within each of the five extant echinoderm classes. Data can be searched by BLASTX or BLASTP or annotation and database identifiers from the Strongylocentrotus purpuratus reference protein set at NCBI.

Bantu Migration

Describing the Bantu Migration using Phylogeographic Analysis

The Bantu migration is a heavily-debated topic that varies depending on the input data used to reconstruct their lineage. This dashboard highlights various migratory paths as well as our combined, less ambiguous migratory model.

About the Janies Lab.

The work of the Janies lab involves empirical studies of organismal diversity. Funded by various government organizations such as DARPA, DTRA, and DOD, the lab develops and manages software to track pathogens and visualize diverse genomic data efficiently.
